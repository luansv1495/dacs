# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful and harassment-free
for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, identity, or ability.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still naming technical concerns clearly.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus criticism on code, design, documentation, and behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after maintainers ask for it to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block
participants who violate this code of conduct.

To report a concern, open a private communication channel with the project
maintainer listed on the repository profile.
